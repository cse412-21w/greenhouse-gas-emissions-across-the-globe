CO2 Emissions Per Capita Map 1992-2014

The third map (blue) shows the distribution of CO2 emissions across the globe by country and controlled by population. Looking over the map, we see that the US, Canada, and Australia along with many of the countries on the Arabian Peninsula consistently have the highest CO2 emissions per capita. As time progresses, the CO2 emissions per capita of North America appear to decrease slightly while the CO2 emissions per capita appear to become less centralized around Russia in Asia. It is worth noting that both China and India have fairly low CO2 emissions per capita.
