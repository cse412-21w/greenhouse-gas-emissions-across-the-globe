This map shows the average yearly temperature in Celsius of different countries since 1743. At the beginning the recordings were only recorded in europe, but as the time passes more countries start to record the temperature and make available as well as the satellites recordings of the temperature. From this graph, it can be seen that the average temperature is slowly raising in each country and that is indicated by the increase of solid red and countries converting from solid blue to faded blue.
