Climate Change and Green House Gas Emissions Across the Globe

By Jeremiah Cheng, Alex Thomson, Steven Wilson, Faisal Alsallum

In the process of thinking what we wanted our project to be about, we all came together to decide with the idea of climate change. What we decided what we wanted to highlight in each of our visualizations was how different factors contributed to the different parts of climate change, specifically when talking about emissions. And how certain countries may affect different aspects of climate change in different ways. And have a good visualization to be able to see correlations between different factors, and factors that might directly affect climate change.
