Overall Introduction

In the process of thinking what we wanted our project to be about, we all came together to decide with the idea of climate change. And have a good visualization to be able to see correlations between different factors, and factors that might directly affect climate change. Hopefull our visualizations givee a better understanding of the different measures of climate change, and certain elements such as CO2 emission that directly affect our earth.
