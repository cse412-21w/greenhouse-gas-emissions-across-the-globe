Total CO2 Emission Map 1992 - 2014

The first map (green) shows how total CO2 emissions are distributed across the globe by country between 1992 and 2014. We see that there are a handful of countries that have considerably higher emissions than the rest of the world. The top two producers are clearly the United States and Russia. India and Russia switch places for third. The United States both starts and ends as one of the largest producers of CO2 throughout the 22 year period.
